Welcome to Nepal

Geography

Nepal is a landlocked country located between China and India, famous for its diverse landscapes, including the Himalayas.

Culture

Nepal boasts a rich cultural heritage with festivals, traditional music, and historic temples that reflect its deep-rooted traditions.

Tourism

Home to Mount Everest, Lumbini (the birthplace of Buddha), and beautiful trekking trails, Nepal is a top destination for adventurers.
